Ground Beef Curry Noodle

Description

This recipe is packed with flavor and a slight Mexican flair. It cooks up in 30 minutes start to finish and only uses one pan.. .no sink full of dirty dishes! I also love that it's family friendly and budget friendly as well!

ingredient

  • ground beef
  • onion
  • red bell pepper
  • garlic
  • beef broth
  • rice
  • Rotel
  • Worcestershire sauce
  • Cheddar cheese
  • salt & pepper

Directions

  1. In a large skillet, brown the ground beef and drain. While the meat is browning, chop the onion and bell pepper.
  2. Add onion, pepper, garlic, and salt & pepper to the meat and stir. Cook for 3 minutes, stirring often.
  3. Add garlic, beef broth, rice, Rotel, and Worcestershire and stir to mix. Cover and simmer 20 minutes or until rice is tender.
  4. Remove from heat and stir in cheese. Serve garnished with chopped parsley if you'd like to get fancy!
